About me

My name is aitor, I am a webdeveloper and graphic designer with a bachelor in web development from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology. My main area of expertise is front end development but i am comfortable with both front and back end development. On top of this I am a graphic designer on the side and i've been running an instagram page since about late 2017 where I post my work. When it comes to graphic design im self taught and often try to incorporate my 3D work into them.

I'm naturally a very curious person and such usually enjoy researching alot of different topics. Outside of WebDev and graphic design I am also really into fashion, photography, music and architecture. These are topics that I spend alot of time with and as such are all inspirations in my design practice. Of course I have interests outside of these and I've spent quite some time doing other random things like building keyboards, researching rare vehicles or learning about cryptography. I have a blog where i share my findings and opinions on these other, more niche interests.
